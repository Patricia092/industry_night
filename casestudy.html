CASE STUDY

BILLY BEER BRAND REFRESH IDP3 Final Integrated Project The project was aimed at revamping the branding and online presence of the alcoholic beverage “Billy Beer.”

STUDENT INFORMATION AND BACKGROUND

Name

SKILLS AND TOOLS

PROJECT GOALS

  • 1. Elevate the brand image of Billy Beer to attract a wider and more loyal customer base.
  • 2. Improve user engagement and satisfaction through an intuitive and visually appealing website.
  • 3. Increase online sales and brand recognition.
  • 4. Establish a strong online presence in the competitive alcoholic beverage market.

SSTAGE THREE

Stage Three: UX/UI Design Phase Wireframing — Designed the website layout and user flow. Visual Design — Crafted a fresh look for Billy Beer, including a new logo and color scheme. Responsive Design — Ensured the website works well on all devices. Prototype — Built interactive models to test the website’s usability.
