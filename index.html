Are you an aspiring Interactive Media Design student eager to dive into the world of creativity, innovation, and professional connections? Look no further! Our upcoming Industry Night in April is your golden ticket to a night filled with knowledge, inspiration, and networking opportunities like never before.

Industry Night was designed exclusively for Interactive Media Design students, with its primary purpose being ‘to bridge the gap between your academic journey and the real-world industry.’
